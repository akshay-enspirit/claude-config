---
name: plan
description: Spec out a feature before implementing — ask questions, then write the plan
---

Ask any clarifying questions needed before writing the plan. Don't plan against ambiguous requirements.

Once clear, write a concise plan:

## Problem
One paragraph. What exactly is being solved and why.

## Approach
The implementation strategy — what changes, where, and in what order.

## Key Decisions
Non-obvious choices and the reasoning behind them. Skip if everything is straightforward.

## Steps
Ordered list of implementation tasks, each small enough to execute in one go.

## Out of Scope
What we're explicitly not doing in this change.

---
Keep it to one screen. Wait for explicit approval before implementing anything.
